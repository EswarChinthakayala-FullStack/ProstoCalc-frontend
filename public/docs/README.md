# 🦷 ProstoCalc: Price Intelligence Engine for Modern Dentistry

ProstoCalc is a state-of-the-art **Clinical Cost Intelligence Platform** designed to bridge the gap between complex dental procedures and transparent financial estimation. It leverages a hybrid approach combining deterministic clinical formulas with advanced Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide highly accurate, justifiable, and dynamic cost predictions.

---

## 🚀 Core Value Proposition

In traditional dentistry, cost estimation is often static or arbitrary. ProstoCalc transforms this into a **Data-Driven Clinical Decision**, providing:
- **Hybrid Estimation:** Real-time comparison between standard clinical regression and ML-based predictions.
- **Neural Justification:** Human-readable clinical reports generated by AI to justify costs to patients.
- **Risk Analysis:** Predictive modeling for cost escalation and treatment complexity.

---

## 🧠 Algorithmic & AI/ML Architecture

### 1. The v5.0 Ensemble Engine
The system uses a sophisticated **6-algorithm ensemble** for high-fidelity clinical cost intelligence:
- **Algorithm 1: Multivariate Regression (Deterministic):** Ported from clinical standards, modeling non-linear interactions between complexity and materials.
- **Algorithm 2: GBDT (Gradient Boosted Decision Trees):** High-performance boosting models that learn complex patterns (e.g., age-complexity interactions).
- **Algorithm 3: Monte Carlo (Probabilistic):** Runs 10,000 randomized simulations per estimate to model clinical uncertainty and complication risks.
- **Algorithm 4: Bayesian Inference:** Updates estimations based on patient-specific clinical evidence (Age, Hygiene, Urgency).
- **Algorithm 5: KNN (K-Nearest Neighbors):** Adapts to historical treatment patterns using a 7-dimensional similarity matrix.
- **Algorithm 6: Weighted Meta-Learner:** A specialized aggregator that combines all algorithm outputs using confidence-weighted voting.

### 2. Clinical Intelligence (LLM)
- **Engine:** Integrates **Llama-3** and **HuggingFace Transformers** to generate "Neural Clinical Justifications."
- **Functionality:** It converts raw numerical data (₹ amounts, complexity scores) into a professional medical report that addresses the patient by name and explains the clinical necessity of the selected materials and procedures.

### 3. Predictive Analytics
- **Dental Health Score (0-100):** A dynamic index calculated using patient hygiene, age, and urgency levels.
- **Escalation Predictor:** Uses high-urgency and complexity parameters to predict potential cost surges if the procedure is delayed (e.g., "15% surge predicted if delayed >30 days").
- **Confidence Scoring:** Every ML prediction is accompanied by a confidence score based on the variance (standard deviation) between different decision trees.

## 🔄 System Flow & Logic

1.  **Clinical Input:** The clinician enters procedure type, units (teeth), sessions, bio-complexity, and material grade.
2.  **Ensemble Simulation:** The `CostEstimatorService` runs an immediate 6-algorithm ensemble simulation (Regression, GBDT, Monte Carlo, Bayesian, KNN).
3.  **Cloud Analysis:** The Backend mirrors this ensemble logic for server-side verification and cloud-grade clinical predictions.
4.  **Enrichment:**
    - AI generates a **Clinical Justification**.
    - The **Escalation Predictor** assesses delay-risks.
    - The **Health Score** is updated.
5.  **Logging & Analytics:** The final "Intelligence Log" is committed to the database, where it serves as training data for future predictions.
